About Us

Welcome to the official website of the San Diego State University ESports Team!

We are a passionate group of students dedicated to competitive gaming and fostering a supportive gaming community on campus.

Our mission is to provide opportunities for students to engage in esports competitions, develop their skills, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Join us in our journey to elevate esports at SDSU!
